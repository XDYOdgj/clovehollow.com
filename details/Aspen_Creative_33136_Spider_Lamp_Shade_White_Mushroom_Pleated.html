Elegant Mushroom Pleated Lamp Shade

This classic mushroom pleated lamp shade brings a refined touch of traditional design to any interior space. Crafted from high-quality tetoron cotton, it offers a soft, warm, and natural glow that enhances lighting ambiance. The white color gives a fresh and clean aesthetic, while its pleated style adds a unique flair to your decor. The virgin styrene backing board ensures durability, making it crack-free and colorfast for years of use.

Ideal for table lamps, vintage lamps, or even kid's room lamps, this versatile shade complements a range of lighting setups. The spider fitter allows for a secure and easy connection to your lamp harp, making it a great match for existing fixtures. Whether you're looking to style a cozy living room, a classic bedroom, or a modern office, this lamp shade blends timeless elegance with contemporary materials for a functional, fashionable look.
