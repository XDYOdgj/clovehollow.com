Primitive Battery Operated Taper Candles with Timer

These flameless taper candles bring a touch of elegance and warmth to any space, offering a safe and stylish alternative to traditional candles. The realistic 3D wick design creates a convincing flickering flame effect, perfect for setting the mood during romantic evenings or festive gatherings.

Each candle is equipped with a built-in 6-hour timer that automatically turns them on and off, eliminating the need for manual control. This makes them ideal for special occasions such as Halloween, Christmas, and Thanksgiving, as well as weddings, parties, and home decor.

With 2 AA batteries included, you can enjoy up to 6 hours of warm lighting and 18 hours of darkness. They are perfect for both indoor and outdoor use, adding a gentle glow to mantles, windowsills, and tabletops without the risk of fire or wax dripping.

The elegant white plastic construction ensures these candles maintain a clean, timeless look that blends seamlessly with any decor style. Whether you're looking to create a cozy ambiance or enhance the aesthetic of your celebration, these candles deliver unmatched charm and convenience.
